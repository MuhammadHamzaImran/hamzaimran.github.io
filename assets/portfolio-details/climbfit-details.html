UI Design and Flutter Development for ClimbFit

ClimbFit is an innovative wall climbing app designed to project digital wall designs onto physical walls using Bluetooth. Users can create accounts, design walls, and publish them for others to use. The app allows users to project these designs to physical walls in gyms or at home, with LEDs lighting up to display the design for climbers to practice.

The app includes features such as the ability to rate and review walls, track climbing history, and discover popular designs. Additionally, climbers can monitor their progress and see how many walls they've completed.

The app was built using Flutter and Firebase, ensuring smooth user experience and real-time synchronization with the backend. Bluetooth technology was integrated to connect the app with physical walls, enabling seamless projection of designs and feedback. This project combined innovative hardware integration with mobile app development to create a unique user experience in the climbing community.
