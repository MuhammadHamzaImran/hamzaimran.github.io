Movie Tracker App

The Movie Tracker app is designed to enhance the movie and TV show experience by providing users with a comprehensive platform to explore, discover, and track their favorite content. It offers users the ability to discover trending movies and TV shows, access detailed information like cast and crew, and explore user-generated reviews and ratings.

Key features include a personalized watchlist to track movies and shows of interest, powerful search functionality to find content by title, actor, or director, and a curated list of top-rated movies. The app ensures a seamless and ad-free experience, allowing users to enjoy content discovery without distractions.

With an intuitive interface and an easy-to-navigate design, the Movie Tracker app provides an engaging platform for movie and TV enthusiasts to stay informed, plan their next viewing, and dive deeper into the world of entertainment.
