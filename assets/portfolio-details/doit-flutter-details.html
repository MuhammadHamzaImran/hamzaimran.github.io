Task Management Mobile App Development Using Flutter and SQLite Database

Introduction: In March 2022, I created a task management mobile app as a personal project. The app had a duration of 2 months and was designed to help users manage their tasks, categories, and app themes. The app also incorporated a local database feature to ensure that users didn't lose their data even after closing the app.

App Features: The task management mobile app was developed using Flutter and SQLite database. The app featured a user-friendly interface that allowed users to create, edit, and delete tasks. Users could also categorize their tasks based on priority or type. The app also allowed users to customize the look and feel of the app using different themes.

Database Management: The app incorporated a local SQLite database to ensure that users' data was saved locally on their devices. This feature enabled users to access their tasks even when they were offline. The database management feature also enabled users to back up their data and restore it if necessary.

Conclusion: Developing the task management mobile app was a challenging but rewarding experience. The project allowed me to hone my skills in Flutter and database management. The app's user-friendly interface and the database management feature were crucial in ensuring that users had a seamless task management experience.
