UI Design and Flutter Development Internship at Assiraat

During my internship at Assiraat, I was tasked with developing complete UI designs and creating app frontends using Flutter. My primary role was that of a UI designer and Flutter developer, where I was responsible for ensuring that the apps were both visually appealing and functional. To accomplish this, I used various tools, including Figma for designing and Flutter for developing.

One of the most significant challenges I faced during my internship was creating a mobile app that was compatible with the website. I had to use online APIs to achieve this, which required me to have a good understanding of the APIs' functionality and how to integrate them with my code. By the end of my internship, I had developed my skills significantly, particularly in creating user-friendly UI designs and developing mobile apps with Flutter.

Overall, my internship at Assiraat was an invaluable experience that allowed me to work on various projects and develop new skills. I worked collaboratively with a team of developers and designers, received regular feedback and guidance from my supervisors, and was able to apply my knowledge to real-world projects. I am confident that the skills I gained during this internship will be an asset in my future career as a UI designer and Flutter developer.
