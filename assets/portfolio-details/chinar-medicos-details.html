UI Design and Flutter Development for Chinar Medicos Ed-Tech App

The Chinar Medicos Ed-Tech app was developed to provide a platform for medical students, offering specialized courses and mock tests to help them prepare for medical competitive exams. The project involved designing a user-friendly interface and developing the app frontend using Flutter.

Figma was used to design the UI, ensuring a smooth and intuitive experience for students accessing the platform. The app included features like course listings, exam preparation tools, and mock tests to help users improve their skills. Flutter was used to ensure that the app was responsive, functional, and visually appealing across both Android and iOS devices.

A significant part of the project involved integrating mock tests and course management systems to ensure seamless access to resources. The app also featured a personalized experience for students, where they could track their progress and performance on mock tests.

This project enhanced my skills in mobile app development with Flutter and UI design, while also allowing me to create an educational tool that serves medical students and aspirants.
